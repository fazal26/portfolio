Roxova.

A media content licensing platform — manage your own catalog, license content in or out, with the legal layer baked in and a pre-licensed library you can opt into directly.

Year2025 — Now
RoleLead Engineer
SectorMedia / Licensing
StatusLive

Problem

// 01

Media creators and businesses needed a single home for content licensing — a place to manage their own catalog for licensing out, request licenses from others, and handle the legal paperwork without juggling spreadsheets, email threads, and PDFs across multiple parties.

Existing options were either heavyweight enterprise software or fragmented tools that handled only one side of the transaction. The brief: one platform, both sides of the deal, with legal compliance built in — not bolted on.

"One platform. Both sides of the deal. Legal compliance built in — not bolted on."

Approach

// 02
  • 01
    Next.js for the surfaceSSR-friendly catalog browse, fast content discovery, and a polished editor for owners to manage their own catalogs.
  • 02
    Node API for the engineModeled content, ownership, licenses, agreements, and parties as first-class objects with clear state transitions and an audit trail.
  • 03
    License request flowDiscover → request → negotiate → agree → deliver. Each step has its own state, notifications, and permission scope.
  • 04
    Pre-licensed libraryA curated content library owners can opt into directly — instant access, no per-deal paperwork, with revenue sharing handled platform-side.
  • 05
    Legal layer, built inContract templates, terms versioning, and agreement history per deal — the boring-but-critical part of licensing, treated as a first-class feature.

Outcome

// 04

Live licensing platform supporting the end-to-end flow from content upload to legal agreement to access delivery. Both creators and licensees operate from one interface — with the legal paperwork handled by the system, not the inbox.
